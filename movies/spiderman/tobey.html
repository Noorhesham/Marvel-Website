Superhuman Strength

Spider-Man's physical strength is enough to lift and launch objects as heavy as most automobiles and break brick walls and break metal chains with ease. It is estimated that he should be able to lift at least 5000 tons. In Spider-Man 3, he was able to drive metal cables into the ground with his extreme strength.
